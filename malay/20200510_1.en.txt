       RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

       MOH please cooperation of all levels and walks of life
       in the fight against the epidemic COVID-19. People need to take
       social responsibility (social responsibility) and have
       Self-control (self-regulation). Ministry of Health would like to advise people
       public to continue practicing the new norm in life:

         • Obeying the Conditional Motion Control Order
          (PKPB),
         • Do not leave the house, except for matters that
          important.
         • Practicing safe social distance (social distancing)
          at least 1 meter from other people
         • Adopt an optimal level of cleanliness at all
          time, such as frequent hand washing using water
          and soap;

       Ministry of Health will continue to monitor developments in the incidence of infection
       in the country, and abroad through information
       obtained from the World Health Organization (WHO).


      The public will be kept informed of any developments
      Last thereon. Similarly, the MOH will ensure steps
      prevention and control measures are implemented
      continuously.
