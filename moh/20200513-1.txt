675 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 13 May 2020

              As of 13 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 675 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Two cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.

2.  We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
