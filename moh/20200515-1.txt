793 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 15 May 2020

  As of 15 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 793 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. There is one Singaporean/ Permanent Resident
case.

2.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
15 MAY 2020
