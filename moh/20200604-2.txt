517 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 04 Jun 2020

1. As of 4 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 517 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 15 cases in the community, of whom two are Singaporeans/Permanent
Residents and 13 are Work Pass holders. All 15 cases were close contacts
of earlier confirmed cases, and have already been placed on quarantine.
They are all asymptomatic, but we had swabbed them to confirm and verify
their status.
2. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
4 JUNE 2020
