752 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 14 May 2020

As of 14 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has preliminarily
confirmed an additional 752 cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore,
the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in foreign
worker dormitories. Two cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent Residents.
We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
