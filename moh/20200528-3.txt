373 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 28 May 2020

                  As of 28 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 373 cases of COVID-19
infection in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit
holders residing in foreign worker dormitories. There are no
Singaporean/ Permanent Resident cases.
2.               We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
