448 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 21 May 2020

         As of 21 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 448 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. 13 cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents. Of these, seven cases were picked up from our active
surveillance and screening of nursing home residents and pre-school
staff, and four are part of a family cluster linked to a dormitory.
Epidemiological investigations are ongoing for the other two cases.
2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
21 MAY 2020
