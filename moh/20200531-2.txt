518 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 31 May 2020

       As of 31 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 518 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are three cases in the community, all of whom are Singaporeans/Permanent
Residents.
2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
