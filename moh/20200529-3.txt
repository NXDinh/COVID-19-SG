611 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 29 May 2020

    As of 29 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 611 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Three cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.
2.   We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
29 MAY 2020
