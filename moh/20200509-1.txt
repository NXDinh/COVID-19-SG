753 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 09 May 2020

      As of 9 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 753 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Nine cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.

2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
