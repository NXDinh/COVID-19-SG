876 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 10 May 2020

1.     As of 10 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 876 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Three cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.
2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
