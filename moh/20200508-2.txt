768 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 08 May 2020

         As of 8 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 768 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Ten cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.

         We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
